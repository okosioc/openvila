Scan Quality Playbook for Static Sites

Date: May 12, 2026

Tags: scan, index, knowledge, maintenance

High quality `/scan` output depends more on source consistency than on prompt tweaks. The best result comes from stable naming, explicit headings, and low duplication.

Use one source of truth per topic

Keep pricing in one file and avoid multiple near-identical copies. Duplicate pages increase ambiguity during document compilation.

Prefer explicit headings

Use direct headings like "Refund Policy", "Support SLA", and "Billing Cycle". These headings are easier for retrieval and improve answer precision.

Update by increment, not rewrite

Small, incremental edits let OpenVila compile only changed docs. This keeps scans fast and makes diff review straightforward.

Review index after each major edit

After policy or pricing changes, run `/scan` and quickly inspect `knowledges/index.md` to confirm that summaries and frequent concerns remain accurate.
