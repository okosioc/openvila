Owner Handoff Checklist Before Go-Live

Date: May 10, 2026

Tags: launch, faq, pricing, policies

Before enabling OpenVila on a production website, the owner should verify that user-facing business pages are complete and easy to parse.

1. Pricing clarity

Every plan should include monthly price, billing cycle, and refund terms. If trial or discount terms exist, write them in one canonical section.

2. Policy readiness

User Agreement and Privacy pages should include effective date, support email, and account termination conditions.

3. FAQ grounding

Add at least 10 common questions, especially onboarding, billing, and cancellation. This usually reduces repetitive tickets after launch.

4. Owner contact fallback

For questions that require manual decisions, provide a clear human-support path so the assistant can escalate without guessing.
